About Us

Rwenzori Savings and Credit Rugendabara is a self-help member-based community organization registered under the Kasese District Local Government. Formed in 2022, it aims to unite subsistence farmers and food market vendors by engaging them in savings and credit initiatives that enhance their economic wellbeing.

The group has grown to over 900 members, with women—especially widows and young mothers—making up 80% of the membership.

Vision

A poverty-free society dedicated to sustainable community development.

Mission

To unite members and uplift their livelihoods through savings, loans, mentorship, and solidarity support.

Leadership Structure

The organization is governed by a General Assembly (all members) and an Executive Committee of 9 elected members who implement community policies.
