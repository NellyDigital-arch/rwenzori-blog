About Us

At Rwenzori SACCO, our mission is to bring people together through a shared commitment to financial empowerment, collective savings, and access to fair credit. We aim to uplift lives by offering mentorship, education, and solidarity-based funding — enabling our members to pursue their goals, support one another, and build a stronger, self-reliant community for generations to come.

The group has grown to over 900 members, with women—especially widows and young mothers—making up 80% of the membership.

Vision

A To create a poverty-free society rooted in equity, dignity, and opportunity — where individuals and communities thrive through sustainable development, financial inclusion, and shared prosperity. We envision empowered members who are not only economically self-reliant but also actively contributing to the growth and well-being of their communities through cooperative values, innovation, and long-term resilience. .

Mission

Mission To unite members and uplift their livelihoods through savings, loans, mentorship, and solidarity support.

Leadership Structure

The organization is governed by a General Assembly (all members) and an Executive Committee of 9 elected members who implement community policies.
