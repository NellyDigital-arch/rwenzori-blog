Our Activities & Services

We empower our community through a variety of impactful initiatives designed to enhance economic stability, especially for women and low-income families.

1. Savings Groups

We facilitate village savings and loan associations (VSLAs) that help members pool resources and support each other financially.

2. Credit Access

We offer accessible, low-interest credit options to help members grow their small businesses or cover emergency needs.

3. Mentorship & Skills Training

We host regular training sessions on entrepreneurship, digital literacy, and financial management—especially for youth and women.

4. Women's Empowerment

With women making up 80% of our membership, we focus heavily on supporting widows, young mothers, and female entrepreneurs with targeted programs.

5. Community Mobilization

We work closely with local leaders and members to promote health, education, and civic engagement across the Rugendabara community.
